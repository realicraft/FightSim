Voting

In FS3, you are capable of voting on features to be added to the game.
While this is not the only way to add features, it is the easiest one for players to use.
Votes end two turns after they start.
Usually, a vote will require a majority, or over half, of votes to be yes votes in order to pass, but some votes require a supermajority, or over two-thirds, of yes votes to pass.
